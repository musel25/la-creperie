Our Story

La Crêperie was born from a love of Brittany's coastal crêperies — where every crêpe is made slow, with care, and enjoyed even slower.

We use stone-ground buckwheat flour for our galettes, fresh-milled wheat for our sweet crêpes, and source every topping from local farms and artisan producers.

Come in for breakfast, lunch, or a sweet afternoon break. Every hour is a good crêpe hour.
